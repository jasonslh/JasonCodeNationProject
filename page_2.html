(1.2.3) Assess the strengths and weaknesses of your final project - are you happy with your website?

The key strengths of this project is the colour scheme and how visually eye catching it is, the simplicity of the overall structure is something that I always have preferred in website design, an overly complex interface is a no go, I opted for ease of use and accessibility when it came to designing the structure of the website. However the simple structure of it may be too simple and not be as unique as I originally envisioned my website to be at the start of the project. I also think that the quality of responses to some questions is variable, I feel that some questions I gave a decent structured answers with variety in language, others not so much. I believe that it is due to my uncertainty of what some questions were asking or if the questions themselves were too broad. Although I like the overall colour scheme and what's going on, I think that the background itself is perhaps too distracting, I want the main focus of the website to be the questions and responses, perhaps adjusting the opacity of the background or have static images may be the key solution here. Despite this, I adjusted the background of each question and answer so that is has a subtle transparency, you can still see the background running behind the text and the text itself is still visible enough to read. Experimentation with the design and layout to promote and more unique website is something I'd like to have a go at changing if given more time and finally I'd attempt to go more in depth with some of my answers.
